Welcome to SVECW

Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women (SVEW) is an esteemed institution located in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established in 2001 with the mission to provide high-quality education to women in the field of engineering and technology. The college is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK), and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). SVEW offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines of engineering, including Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical, Civil, Mechanical,Artificial Intelligence and Datascience ,Machine Learning and Information Technology. The college is well-known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong industry connections, which provide students with numerous opportunities for career growth.
